Still

Focus sessions with Screen Time shields, plus optional alarms you dismiss by walking or scanning your own QR code.

Purpose

Still is built for people who want to reduce distractions on their own terms: you choose which apps, categories, and websites are limited during a timed focus session. Alarms are optional and designed so you finish waking up with a short walk or a sticker you place away from your bed.

Highlights

  • Timed focus using Apple’s Screen Time APIs (Family Controls, Managed Settings, Device Activity)
  • Optional alarms: system alarms on supported iOS versions, with walk-to-dismiss or QR dismiss
  • Data stays on your device for core focus features; see our privacy summary
